Fynd Privacy Policy

We believe your data belongs to you. Here is exactly how we collect, use, and protect it.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Fynd ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-assisted travel discovery app that helps users create personalised trip plans, discover nearby places, and manage travel itineraries. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through the Fynd mobile application.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@fynd.app

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Full name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information: Home city and profile photo (optional)
  • Travel preferences: Vibe selections (e.g. arts, food, nightlife) and travel style preferences
  • Trip data: Destinations, saved places, and itineraries you create
  • Feedback and support: Messages sent through the Support & Feedback form

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location: GPS coordinates when you use map features, Service Hub, or nearby place search (only when you grant permission)
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes
  • App usage data: Screens visited, features used, and session duration via Firebase Analytics
  • Error reports: Crash logs and error events via Sentry for app stability improvement

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Places API: Place details, photos, and ratings for search results
  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google
  • Stripe: Payment processing for Fynd Plus subscriptions. We never see or store your full card details — Stripe handles all payment data

4. AI-Generated Content

⚠️ When you create a trip, your destination, vibe selections, and preferences are sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalised itineraries. No personally identifiable information (name, email, location) is included in these requests. OpenAI processes this data under their own Privacy Policy.

AI-generated suggestions are based on pattern recognition and may not always be accurate. Always exercise personal judgment when visiting recommended places.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted service providers, strictly to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, and analytics — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: AI itinerary generation (anonymised trip data only) — OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API: Place search and map data — Google Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions — Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Crash reporting for app stability — Sentry Privacy Policy

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Itineraries and saved places are retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Crash logs and analytics data are retained for up to 12 months
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 7 business days

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information via Account Settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data via Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to data processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke location permission at any time in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fynd.app or use the Support & Feedback form in the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Fynd is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase security rules restrict database access to authenticated users only
  • Passwords are never stored — Firebase Authentication handles all credential management
  • Payment details are never stored by Fynd — handled exclusively by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure

10. International Transfers

Fynd operates globally. Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. Where required, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards for international data transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Fynd after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: privacy@fynd.app
  • In-app: Profile → Support & Feedback
